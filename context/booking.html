Property Highlights

Situated in Lusaka, the recently renovated Gracimor Hyndland Estate features accommodation 18 km from Lusaka National Museum and 32 km from Lusaka South Country Club. The air-conditioned accommodation is 15 km from Lusaka Golf Club, and guests can benefit from private parking available on site and free WiFi. The property is non-smoking and is set 35 km from Chilanga Golf Club.

The spacious apartment features 3 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms, bed linen, towels, a flat-screen TV with streaming services, a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen, and a patio with garden views. The unit at the property features a hot tub and a dressing room. For added privacy, the accommodation features a private entrance. Guests can relax in the garden at the property.
